Project 04

BidSignal

GovCon Decision Intelligence

Created March 2026 Last updated June 24, 2026

AI-assisted government-contracting decision engine for small defense contractors.

BidSignal helps small contractors discover realistic federal opportunities, compare SAM.gov notices against workspace targeting, inspect USAspending-backed market signals, and decide whether to bid, review, watch, pass, prime, team, or subcontract. Scores support the decision — they do not replace capture judgment or guarantee awards.

  • SAM.gov discovery with Postgres-backed inventory (549 catalog rows; 196 Discover-visible open opportunities validated June 2026)
  • Workspace targeting profile with NAICS and fit signals
  • Discover ranking with scope fit, bid effort, and recommended action
  • Opportunity detail with bid reality and official-source verification
  • CMMC/CUI/DFARS bid-risk screening — read-path Phase 0 only
  • Today's Queue for time-sensitive pursuit decisions
  • Stripe + Clerk-ready private beta foundation (326 tests passing, 2 skipped)

Status: Private beta / launch candidate — local and invite-only demo. No public SaaS deployment yet. Repository is private.
